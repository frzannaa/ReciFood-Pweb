Oreo Milkshake

Ingredients :
- 6 cups vanilla ice cream
- 1/4 cup milk
- 1/3 cup chocolate saucer
- 12 oreos
- Whipped cream , optional, for topping
Directions :
- Combine ice cream, milk, chocolate sauce, and Oreos in a blender and blend until combined.
- Top with whipped cream.

Strawberry Lime Punch Recipe

Ingredients :
- 1 cup vodka
- 1/4 cup lime juice
- 30 ml strawberry crusher
- 250 ml concentrated lemonade
- 1 cup ice cubes
- 1/2 cup strawberry
- lime zest as required
Directions :
- Mix all the ingredients in a bowl except strawberries
- Take a large bowl and combine concentrated lemonade, vodka, lime juice and strawberry crusher in it.
- Mix well using a stirrer
- Add sliced strawberries in the cocktail and serve fresh
- Take a chilled glass and add crushed ice cubes into it.
- Pour the drink into it and add freshly sliced strawberries to it
- Stir well and garnish with lime zest, serve chilled.

Grilled pineapple mojitos

Ingredients :
- 4 limes, thinly sliced
- 1 bunch mint leaves, plus sprigs to serve
- 1 cup (250ml) white rum
- 2 tbs honey
- Crushed ice, to serve
- 1 tbs honey
- Juice of 2 limes
- 1 pineapple (skin on), sliced lengthways into 1cm wedges, core removed
- 1 tbs white rum
Directions :
- For the grilled pineapple, combine the honey, white rum and lime juice in a large bowl.
- Add pineapple, turn to coat and stand for 10 minutes.
- Heat chargrill pan over medium heat.
- Add the pineapple and cook for 1-2 minutes each side until charred.
- Transfer to a tray. Chill until cold.
- Set aside 8 pineapple wedges. Remove and discard skin from the remaining pineapple and roughly chop.
- Divide chopped pineapple, lime, mint, rum and honey among 8 x 300ml glasses.
- Use a muddler or rolling pin to roughly bruise. Top each glass with soda water and ice.
- Garnish mojitos with reserved pineapple wedges and extra mint. Serve immediately.

Green Smoothie

Ingredients :
- 1/4 cup milk (water, soy milk, or almond milk also work)
- Juice of 1 orange , freshly squeezed
- 5 Ice cubes
- 2 large handfuls fresh spinach leaves
- 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
- 1 banana.
- 1/2 cup frozen fruit , pineapple, strawberries, mango, peaches, or raspberries
- 2 teaspoons ground flaxseed , optional
-chia seeds , optional
Directions :
- Add liquid (milk and fresh orange juice) to the bottom of your blender. Also add ice cubes.
- Next add spinach (or other greens).
- Add Greek yogurt.
- Next add your frozen fruit and additional add ins (flaxseed, chia seeds, etc).
- Blend until smooth. Check consistency and add more liquid depending on your preference.
